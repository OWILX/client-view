President’s Remark

A message from the President of FUOMSA

Comrade Amodu Oluwadunsin Ayomide

PKA Perfect Paul of FUOYE

President, FUOMSA

On behalf of the Executive Council and the entire FUOMSA family, I warmly welcome you to our official platform.

The Federal University Oye-Ekiti Medical Students’ Association (FUOMSA) remains a unified body committed to promoting healthcare delivery through collaboration, innovation, and research.

Under the current administration, fondly referred to as The Utopian Cabinet, we are driven by a shared vision to strengthen academic excellence, student welfare, and community health impact.

We sincerely acknowledge the unwavering support and leadership of the College of Medicine, led by the Provost, the Dean of Basic Medical Sciences, our distinguished Heads of Departments, the College Secretary, and all academic and administrative leaders whose guidance continues to shape our journey.

As an association, FUOMSA recognizes the need for sustainable development. We therefore call on alumni, partners, organizations, and well-meaning individuals to support our initiatives through donations, sponsorships, and collaborations that will enable us to better serve our members and the community at large.

Together, we can build a stronger FUOMSA that stands as a model of excellence, innovation, and service.

Comrade Amodu Oluwadunsin Ayomide
President, FUOMSA
