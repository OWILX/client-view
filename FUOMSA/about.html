About FUOMSA

Advancing medical education, research, and healthcare delivery through collaboration and innovation.

Who We Are

The Federal University Oye-Ekiti Medical Students’ Association (FUOMSA) represents medical students committed to academic excellence, ethical practice, and community-oriented healthcare.

We provide a platform for collaboration, leadership development, innovation, and research among future healthcare professionals.

Our Purpose


Our Mission

To promote quality healthcare delivery through collaboration, innovation, and research among medical students.

Our Vision

To be a leading medical students’ association driving excellence in healthcare and medical research.

Our Values

Integrity, teamwork, professionalism, innovation, and service to humanity.

Our Journey
